# About Me

Hi, I'm **Calabrimbor** (Callum). I am a game server developer and programmer specializing in building high-performance, automated, and deeply optimized multiplayer environments.

My work primarily revolves around **Minecraft** split between configuring intricate server infrastructure and scripting custom mechanics.

---

## 🛠️ What I Do

My development philosophy centers on **performance engineering** and **infrastructure automation**.

---

## 🚀 My Philosophy: True Community Play

I believe that multiplayer gaming thrives when it is driven by the community, not by monetization.

Every server environment and tool I develop is designed from the ground up to be completely free of pay-to-win mechanics or premium paywalls. My goal is to deliver professional-grade, high-performance environments built purely for the love of the game and the community around it.
